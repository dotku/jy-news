This event marks the first time the Nemotron Developer Day has been held in Korea, previously it was only held at the GTC conference. At that time, local Korean artificial intelligence developers, researchers, and ecosystem leaders will gather to conduct in-depth technical exchanges. Discussions and practical innovation activities on autonomous artificial intelligence will last for two days.

The event will kick off with a keynote speech by Bryan Catanzaro, NVIDIA's Vice President of Applied Research, titled "Researcher Toolkit: Open Models, Datasets, and Technologies."
