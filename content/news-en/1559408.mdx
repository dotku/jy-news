In terms of core performance, the ThinkPad X14 AI 2026 is equipped with up to an Intel Core Ultra X9 processor, featuring a 16-core and 16-thread architecture, balancing performance and power efficiency. Paired with an Intel Arc B390 GPU and high-speed memory, it handles daily tasks like Office suite, multiple browser tabs, design, and video editing with ease, offering smooth multitasking without lag.

Even more impressive is its AI computing power, reaching up to 180 TOPS, effectively supporting various AI functions and perfectly adapting to future smart office trends.

Regarding the screen, the ThinkPad X14 AI 2026 features a 2.8K high refresh rate true color screen, boasting ultra-high resolution, 100% DCI-P3 professional-grade color gamut, and a dynamic refresh rate of 30Hz~120Hz. Combined with a 91% screen-to-body ratio, it provides a wider field of view and an immersive experience.

In terms of battery life, the ThinkPad X14 AI 2026 is equipped with a 74Wh high-density large-capacity battery, offering up to 15.5 hours of battery life under testing conditions and up to 37 hours of video playback. It can easily support a full day of use without plugging in under normal office workloads.

Furthermore, the ThinkPad X14 AI 2026 features the Tianxi AI personal super intelligence agent, bringing a complete suite of smart office functions, simplifying complex tasks and automating repetitive ones. It supports a wide range of practical AI functions, including AI writing, AI search, AI translation, and AI control.

This laptop also features Wi-Fi 7, enabling fast transmission of meetings, cloud documents, and large files. It also supports an external 4G LTE module, ensuring stable and reliable network connectivity to handle unexpected situations.

In terms of ports, the ThinkPad X14 AI 2026 retains a variety of practical ports, including dual Thunderbolt 4, dual USB-A, HDMI 2.0, and a 3.5mm audio jack, making it convenient to connect USB drives, monitors, projectors, and docks. Business presentations, conference room projection, and data transfer can all be done in one step, truly covering all office scenarios.
