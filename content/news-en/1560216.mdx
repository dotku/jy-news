Currently, "The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild" and "The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom" have landed on the Switch 2 platform. Combined with the development details previously revealed by Monolith Soft, the new work plans for the "Legend of Zelda" series have once again sparked heated discussion among players. Leaker Nash Weedle released a cryptic message hinting at the release of a new installment in the series in 2027, coupled with clues from previously leaked project code names, making this rumor more credible.

According to the breakdown of the leaked information, multiple works in the "Legend of Zelda" series each have their own exclusive internal code names, and the code name "Bernard" specifically refers to this unannounced 2D new sequel.

At this stage, this content has not been officially confirmed by Nintendo. There is no definite information regarding the new game's art style and gameplay framework. Please refer to official announcements for further information.
