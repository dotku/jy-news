In terms of appearance, the new car is basically consistent with the already launched CLA 300 L (starting at 249,000 yuan), adopting the family design language, equipped with through-type LED daytime running lights, and the latest three-pointed star light source inside the headlights on both sides. The front face features a closed grille with a starry sky mesh (142 luminous stars) and a large Mercedes-Benz LOGO. The body side adopts a smooth, sloping back coupe shape.

In terms of body size, the new car measures 4763/1836/1471mm in length, width, and height, with a wheelbase of 2830mm and a curb weight of 2005kg. In terms of power, it is equipped with a rear-mounted single motor with a maximum power of 165 kW, matched with a 60kWh lithium iron phosphate battery pack and an electric two-speed gearbox. The CLTC pure electric range is 600 kilometers, and the maximum speed is 200km/h.
