Yunfei stated that this was actually a certain uninformed person from a certain manufacturer privately contacting "Principal," and it wasn't a manufacturer trying to target Geekwan.

This response broke previous speculation of "manufacturers jointly pressuring for video removal," directing the blame towards the private operation of individual executives of certain manufacturers, while also revealing the internal stance differences within digital manufacturers regarding review content.

According to previous reports, in February of this year, Geekwan released a video titled "Mobile Game Performance Daxiongping: Manufacturers Cheating is Too Crazy!", which was unexpectedly removed from all platforms, attracting attention from netizens and the media.

After the incident, Geekwan revealed that they did not receive any official notification or infringement explanation when the video was removed, and the entire matter was not handled through normal procedures.
