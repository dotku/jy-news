The front features a 1.5-inch LTPO AMOLED flexible screen with a resolution of 466x466. Bezels are reduced by 36%, and the screen-to-body ratio is increased by 11.6%, with a peak brightness of 3000 nits.

The strap is available in titanium alloy and composite leather materials, accommodating wrist circumferences of 140-210mm.

Regarding the earbuds, each weighs approximately 4 grams and supports adaptive recognition, not distinguishing between left and right ears. They can be used independently in single or dual-ear mode, and support transparency and noise cancellation modes.

The earbuds are equipped with a surrounding charging ring, allowing them to begin charging when placed back into the watch on any side. In terms of battery life, with noise cancellation enabled, a full charge provides 3 hours of music playback, and 4 hours with noise cancellation disabled. The watch offers a total battery life of 3 days.

Health monitoring functions include pulse wave atrial fibrillation and premature beat alerts, sleep apnea detection, high blood sugar risk assessment research, heart health research, and multidimensional emotional health monitoring.

Additionally, it supports digital car keys, Alipay tap-to-pay, and is compatible with both iOS and Android phones.

Pricing: The Amber Brown (brown vegan leather composite strap) and Obsidian Black (black vegan leather composite strap) versions are priced at 3488 yuan, while the Titanium Space Silver (titanium-colored titanium metal strap) version is priced at 3988 yuan. Sales begin on April 25th at 10:08, with a limited-time gift of HUAWEI Care+ service worth 299 yuan (including one accidental damage protection, one earbud loss protection service, and one year of battery rejuvenation service).
