This marks the first time a Chinese motorcycle brand has reached the top podium of a world-class event, ending the 37-year monopoly of European, American, and Japanese brands.

Driven by his passion for motorcycles, Zhang Xue started as a repair apprentice at the age of 14, and later became a professional motorcycle racer, worked with Kaiyue Motorcycle, and founded Zhang Xue Motorcycle, ultimately creating the motorcycle that won the world championship.

His early story has also become widely known, serving as a model for achieving dreams through success.

In 2006, 19-year-old Zhang Xue demonstrated his motorcycle skills to reporters from Hunan Satellite TV's "Evening News" program in order to attract the attention of a team, shouting, "Chase your dreams, because courage makes my life more wonderful."
