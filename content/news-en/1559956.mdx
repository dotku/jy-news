She didn't realize she had bought the wrong tickets until she arrived at the venue and Guang Liang took the stage. The woman then shared this blunder online, unexpectedly gaining a lot of attention.

Warmly, Xu Liang's team contacted her after seeing the related video and gifted her a free ticket to Xu Liang's May Day concert, leaving many netizens envious.

According to official information, Xu Liang's "Time Folding" tour will land in Chengdu during the May Day holiday, from May 1st to 5th, with five consecutive performances at the Chengdu Phoenix Mountain Sports Park Comprehensive Gymnasium.

Data shows that Xu Liang was born in Qingdao, Shandong in 1987 and is a well-known original singer and musician. In 2010, he became popular online with singles such as "Bad Girl" and "Guest, You Can't," and his iconic youth ballads and duet style became the youth memories of a generation. He officially signed and debuted in 2011, and later gradually transitioned behind the scenes, creating the music brand "One Inch of Light Years" and producing many popular hit singles such as "Changeable" and "I Will Wait."
