Based on the 733 employees mentioned in the developer's 2025 annual business report, the total expenditure exceeds 17 million yuan, a significant sum.

Pearl Abyss CEO stated in an internal letter: “I express my deep respect and gratitude to every colleague who created a product passionately pursued by the world, and I am awarding a celebratory bonus for reaching 5 million sales to all employees diligently fulfilling their duties in their respective positions.”

He also emphasized: “Our journey does not end here, and the potential confirmed today will be the strongest driving force to overcome any future challenges.”

"Red Desert" was announced in 2019, with a development cycle lasting 7 years, and officially released this year. Initial reviews were mixed, but Pearl Abyss quickly addressed core issues such as control schemes and equipment storage through frequent major updates, resulting in a turnaround in reputation.

Currently, the game has an 86% positive rating on Steam (based on 51,616 user reviews), with a peak concurrent player count of 276,261.
