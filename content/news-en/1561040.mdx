Regarding pricing, the standard edition of "Forza Horizon 6" on the Chinese Steam store is priced at 298 yuan, the deluxe edition at 398 yuan, and the premium edition at 498 yuan. Players who purchase the premium edition will receive early access to the game content 4 days in advance.

The game is set in Japan and features the largest map in the series to date. The Tokyo area will be 5 times larger than Guanajuato in "Forza Horizon 5" and includes multiple uniquely characterized administrative districts.

In terms of content, the game will launch with over 550 cars and introduces a new tire wear system that presents more realistic wear effects based on driving conditions.

Furthermore, the new installment has comprehensively upgraded driving details, including more refined steering animations and a steering wheel turning angle of up to 540 degrees, further enhancing handling realism and immersion.
