Shift Up has released a commemorative art piece for the 2nd anniversary of 《Sword and Fairy Together》. The official statement indicates they have “exciting things” planned for the future of 《Sword and Fairy Together》 and encourage players to stay tuned.

The sequel, 《Sword and Fairy Together 2》, will be upgraded to the Unreal Engine 5 and will launch on multiple platforms, no longer being exclusive to the PS5.
