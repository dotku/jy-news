From the display of the "My Moments" page, the display position of the Moments text has changed from being on the right side of the picture to above the picture; the display format of the publication date has also been adjusted. In addition, a "Moments Album" entry has been added to the upper right corner of the page, allowing you to switch and zoom in and out from the perspective of "Year," "Month," and "Day," similar to the display format of the Apple Photos app.

Regarding the change in the Moments page layout style, Tencent Customer Service stated that the change in text position may be an optimization adjustment of the display form, and they will continue to optimize the browsing experience.
