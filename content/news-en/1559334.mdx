The livestream footage showed the vehicle traveling on a highway, with only the vehicle model displayed on the central control screen, and critical information such as lane lines and other vehicles completely disappeared.

Notably, after the navigation interface bug appeared, Zero Auto immediately closed the comment section of the livestream, preventing users from interacting. In the livestream footage, Zero Auto CEO Cao Li also frequently looked at the central control screen with a slightly anxious expression.

Some Zero Auto owners have reported that this issue of AR navigation interface lane lines disappearing is not the first time it has occurred, and is a common bug for the model. Some owners have said they have encountered similar problems two or three times.

As of now, Zero Auto has not yet made an official response regarding the navigation anomaly issue during the livestream.
