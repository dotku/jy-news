FSD

Tesla CEO Elon Musk had previously predicted that FSD could receive commercial licensing as early as February of this year. Despite receiving partial approval and launching pilot testing more than a year ago, a nationwide rollout in China has been delayed, hindering the company's ability to leverage this key growth engine.

However, Tesla FSD recently received a key approval in the Netherlands, which will allow Tesla to accelerate the rollout of the technology in Europe.
