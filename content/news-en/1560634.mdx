"Mother Mary" is positioned as a psychological, sexy, pop-thriller, blending music, fashion, and supernatural elements with a bizarre and gorgeous style. The film tells the story of legendary pop superstar Mother Mary suddenly ending her tour after an emotional breakdown. When she prepares to return to the stage with a comeback concert, she seeks out a long-estranged friend, and former costume designer Sam Anselm (played by Michaela Coel), to create a new and perfect stage image. Their long-awaited reunion brings them back into each other's lives, and buried emotions and scars gradually resurface.
