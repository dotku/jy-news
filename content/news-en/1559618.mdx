It is reported that Grecia's sister filed a report on the 12th, stating that her sister went missing in southeastern Mexico. Witnesses claimed to have seen people suspected of carrying weapons abducting Grecia in a small town. Police initially suspected the disappearance was related to a bar attack incident in the area.

After four days of searching, police finally found Grecia near a highway, fortunately she was unharmed. The search committee revealed that it took several days to find her because the photo used in the missing person notice was heavily edited, resulting in a significant discrepancy between the person and the photo, which delayed her timely discovery.
