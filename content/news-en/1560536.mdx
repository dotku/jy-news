Now WeChat stickers support sending and viewing original images. OPPO and WeChat have collaborated to allow sharing of 200 million pixel super-clear images within stickers.

Android users updating to version 8.0.71 and above can experience this feature, while iOS is still being worked on.

This upgrade to WeChat sticker image quality aligns with the current trend of high-pixel phone photography and caters to users' demand for high-definition sharing.

On April 21st, the OPPO Find X9s Pro was officially released, featuring a new dual 200 million pixel camera system, with both the main and telephoto lenses upgraded to 200 million pixel level.

At the launch event, OPPO announced a deep collaboration with WeChat to improve connectivity within the social ecosystem. Now, users can directly send 200 million pixel super-clear photos through WeChat stickers, and support one-click viewing of the original image and sharing to Moments.
