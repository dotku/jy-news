"Elden Ring" will star Cailee Spaeny, who has appeared in "Alien: Romulus" and "Knives Out 3."

In addition to Cailee Spaeny, the following actors will also star in the live-action "Elden Ring" movie. The roles each actor will play have not yet been officially announced.

Ben Whishaw, "James Bond" series

Tom Burke, "Furiosa: A Mad Max Saga"

Havana Rose Liu

Sonoya Mizuno, "Ex Machina"

Jonathan Pryce, "Red Alert 3"

Ruby Cruz

Nick Offerman, "The Last of Us"

John Hodgkinson, "Napoleon"

Jefferson Hall, "Oppenheimer"

Emma Laird, "28 Days Later: The Host"

Peter Serafinowicz, "Guardians of the Galaxy"

Cailee Spaeny, "Alien: Romulus"
