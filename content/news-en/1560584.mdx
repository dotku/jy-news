Xu Dongdong frankly stated that actors often don't have complete control, and ups and downs, stops and starts are normal in life.

Regarding the labels attached to her, Xu Dongdong said: "Tearable or not."

She believes: "Others may think ambition is about pursuing fame and fortune, but for me, taking care of my family is a deeper ambition."

It is reported that Xu Dongdong was born in Harbin, Heilongjiang Province in 1990. In 2016, she quickly became popular for playing the role of Shen Jiawen, the "big sister-in-law," in the suspenseful crime web drama "Yu Zui."

In 2018, Xu Dongdong stood out for her participation in the Chinese comedy film "Crazy Rich in Xihong City." The popularity of "Crazy Rich in Xihong City" took Xu Dongdong's acting career to the next level, making her a popular female star at the time.

In subsequent roles, Xu Dongdong was labeled as primarily "flaunting sexiness," and works such as "Special Agent Wildflower," "The Return of the Big Sister-in-Law," and "The Boss Lady" continued to reinforce this image, solidifying her position as the "Queen of Online Movies."

Even more well-known is her collaboration with the national beverage brand Coconut Palm Coconut Juice, with netizens bestowing upon her the title of "Coconut Princess."

However, in her Weibo post, she mentioned the origin of her cooperation with Wang Dongshi, Chairman of Coconut Palm Group: "Chairman Wang didn't cooperate with me for so many years because of my fame. I'm not famous, there are many people better than me. I'm a very ordinary actress, I used to be an extra. It's just because of my creativity that they have cooperated with me for so many years."
