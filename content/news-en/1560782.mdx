As of 21:30 on April 30th, the number of reservations for the Home version on JD exceeded 49,000 people, and the number of reservations for the House of the Monsters version, also released simultaneously on JD, exceeded 39,000 people, bringing the total number of reservations for the Pop Mart LABUBU refrigerator on JD to nearly 90,000. The number of reservations for the House of the Monsters version on the Pop Mart Tmall flagship store exceeded 9,000 people.

Amidst the purchasing frenzy, the Pop Mart LABUBU refrigerator, originally priced at 5999 yuan, is now being resold for 9999 yuan, a premium of 4000 yuan.
