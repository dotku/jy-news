Zelnick stated at the Interactive Innovation Conference: “I’m very nervous about the release of every project, and this time, please multiply that feeling by ten billion.”

Zelnick believes that a lack of tension is a danger signal for professionals. Once creators are no longer afraid of failure, they should leave the industry. In his view, being overly confident before truly achieving victory is a sure path to mistakes.

Zelnick said: “I think when you’re no longer feeling that fear, you should probably find another job… because if you’re declaring success before you’ve achieved it, you’re probably wrong.”

Despite this, Zelnick remains optimistic. He believes in the unique teams within the company's studios. His successful strategy is to find the best talent and give them complete freedom and unlimited resources – whether financial, creative, technical, or human – to realize their vision.

Zelnick concluded that the key to success lies in the balance between art and commerce: while developers are creating, the company handles logistics and promotion. He believes that the independence of creativity is the cornerstone of success, and the task of management is to support this mechanism, rather than creating drama and unnecessary interference.

"GTA6" is still planned for release on PS5 and Xbox Series platforms in the fall of 2026.
