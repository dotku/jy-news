The entire series is standardly equipped with CATL batteries, available in 82kWh and 95kWh capacities, offering ranges of 630km, 700km, and 730km respectively.

It can gain 150km of range in 5 minutes, and charging from 10% to 80% takes only 20 minutes.

This vehicle is the first mass-produced model resulting from the technological collaboration between Volkswagen and Xpeng, taking only 24 months from project initiation to launch.

As a key model in Jinbi Volkswagen’s intelligent strategy, the Wiz08 is positioned as a full-size all-electric SUV, nearly 5 meters long with a wheelbase of 3080mm, and adopts a new design language.

The interior features a home-like design style, with a wrap-around cockpit paired with a 10.25-inch high-definition LCD instrument panel and dual 14.96-inch linked screens.

Intelligence is a core highlight, with the new car equipped with Xpeng’s VLA end-to-end large model intelligent driving system, supporting highway and urban NOA (Navigate On Autopilot).

It features full-scene assisted driving from "parking spot to parking spot" and retains Volkswagen’s "human-machine co-driving" design philosophy, ensuring safer and smoother control.

The new car is built on an 800V high-voltage platform, offering both single-motor and dual-motor models.

The chassis is equipped with dual-chamber air suspension, DCC adaptive chassis adjustment, AI intelligent chassis, and magic carpet intelligent predictive function, with front brakes featuring Brembo bidirectional four-piston fixed calipers.

It retains the solid handling feel of German cars while effectively filtering road bumps, balancing handling and comfort.

In terms of safety, the Wiz08 adopts an ultra-high-strength cage body structure, with 80% high-strength steel, and the battery has passed 436 all-scenario safety tests.
