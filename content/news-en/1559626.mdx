The statement pointed out that recently, fake media outlets have published maliciously fabricated screenshots and fabricated false information. The related content is completely untrue and contains obvious, low-level errors that defy common sense. This act of spreading rumors has seriously damaged Xishanju’s legitimate rights and interests and also violates relevant laws and regulations.

Xishanju clearly stated in the statement that the company’s daily operations are normal and all research and development and distribution plans are steadily progressing. It is understood that Xishanju has been deeply involved in the gaming industry for more than 30 years, always focusing on game development and player services. This statement also calls on the public to rationally discern information, taking the content officially released by the company as the standard, and to refrain from creating, believing, or spreading rumors, jointly maintaining a clear and healthy online environment.

Regarding this act of spreading rumors, Xishanju explicitly stated that it will pursue the relevant legal responsibility of those who maliciously create and spread rumors through legal channels, effectively safeguarding its legitimate rights and interests.
