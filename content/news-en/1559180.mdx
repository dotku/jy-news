The sloping "Aero Hatch" design optimizes aerodynamics, aiming for a drag coefficient of 0.263.

In terms of dimensions, the vehicle measures 4155(4170)/1800/1505mm in length, width, and height, with a wheelbase of 2680mm.

The interior follows the "Furnished Space" concept, with a 5-seat layout and a flat floor. The conventional trunk volume is 441L, complemented by a layered center console, stackable cup holders, and other practical designs.

As the first Hyundai model in Europe to feature the Pleos Connect in-car system, it is developed based on Android Automotive OS, offering optional 12.9-inch/14.6-inch large screens, and equipped with Hyundai SmartSense intelligent driving system, Digital Key 2.0, 3.6kW V2L external discharge, and a BOSE sound system.

In terms of power, the IONIQ 3 is equipped with a single motor, with a maximum power of 147 horsepower and a peak torque of 250N・m.

The standard range version is equipped with a 42.2kWh battery, with a WLTP range of 344km; the long range version is equipped with a 61kWh battery, with a WLTP range of 496km.
