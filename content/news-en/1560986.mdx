Figure 1: Bodyguards around Musk

As of February this year, Tesla's security spending on Musk has more than doubled, increasing from $500,000 in the same period last year to $1.3 million.

Figure 2: Musk says he needs to strengthen security

This is not all of Musk's security expenses. Musk's other companies, including SpaceX and xAI, will also bear some security expenses to address risks associated with Musk's high profile.

In September last year, "retail shareholder advocate" Alexandra Merz posted on X that Tesla needed to "significantly" increase spending on Musk's security. Musk responded that he "definitely needs to strengthen security."

The New York Times reported in 2024 that Musk travels with up to 20 bodyguards and a medical professional.

As of press time, Tesla has not commented on this matter.
