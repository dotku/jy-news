According to data released by Alinea Analytics, 13 Xbox first-party games have sold over 100,000 units on the PS5 platform. This indicates the success of Xbox's multi-platform strategy and may suggest that it will be very difficult for Xbox to return to exclusivity.

Alinea Analytics has revealed the performance of Xbox games on PS5. Currently, 13 games have exceeded 100,000 units sold, 3 games have exceeded 1 million units, and total revenue has exceeded $700 million.

These 13 games are:

Forza Horizon 5, 5.8 million

Sea of Thieves, 2.7 million

The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion Remastered, 1.2 million

State of Decay, 770,000

Gears of War: Ultimate Edition, 684,000

Doom Eternal, 567,000

Indiana Jones and the Great Circle, 537,000

Ninja Gaiden 4, 262,000

Microsoft Flight Simulator, 252,000

Starfield, 207,000

Age of Empires II: Definitive Edition, 173,000

The Outer Worlds 2, 163,000

Age of Mythology: Retold, 123,000
