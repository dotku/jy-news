In the finals, Zeng Zhuojun defeated Japanese player Laggia, who had fought his way up from the losers bracket, with an overwhelming performance, successfully defending his EVO Japan championship for the second consecutive time and earning qualification for the 2026 Esports World Cup and SWC. Taiwanese player ZJZ finished in third place.

As one of the most influential fighting game tournaments globally, EVO Japan 2026 was held from May 1st to 3rd, bringing together multiple popular fighting game events, including *Hungry Wolf Legend: City of Wolves*. This year’s *City of Wolves* tournament reached a new high in scale, with over 400 participants and exceptionally fierce competition.

Zeng Zhuojun is one of China’s most representative fighting game players, having won multiple championships at EVO and other international events, and is active in multiple projects such as *King of Fighters* and *Street Fighter*.
