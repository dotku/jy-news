At the event, Zhang Zetian appeared in a gray professional suit, exuding elegance and grace, and demonstrating a mature and capable public image through her speech.

It is reported that on January 26th of this year, Zhang Zetian officially announced the JD Art Museum project on social media, confirming that the JD Art Museum will open in Shenzhen in 2027.

Located in the Shenzhen Bay Super Headquarters Base, it covers a total construction area of over 10,000 square meters and is positioned as a new cultural space "connecting people with art and technology." Designed by an internationally renowned team, it focuses on the deep integration of art and technology, creating a diverse and immersive art experience.

In recent years, Zhang Zetian has continuously deepened her involvement in the intersection of art, philanthropy, and technology. In March of this year, she accompanied Her Highness Sheikha Al Mayassa bint Hamad bin Khalifa Al Thani, Chairperson of Qatar Museums, to inspect art projects related to the JD Art Museum.

In addition, she has frequently appeared at international fashion weeks and art exhibitions in Cannes, London, and other cities, serving as a Guest Artistic Director for VOGUE ART to expand her artistic endeavors. At the same time, Zhang Zetian also launched her personal podcast, "Xiao Tian Zhang."
