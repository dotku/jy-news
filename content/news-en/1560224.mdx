It is foreseeable that with the rapid evolution of technology, future robots will become more intelligent, flexible, and possess emotional attributes. They will integrate better and faster into people's daily lives, becoming valuable assistants in industrial production, elderly care, and public services.

The continuous expansion of robot application scenarios, from replacing strenuous physical labor to participating in leisure and entertainment, is essentially a reflection of the further release of human productivity. In the future, this new model of human-robot coexistence will create more possibilities for society and profoundly reshape our lifestyles and work patterns.
