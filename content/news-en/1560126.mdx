This dagger holds extraordinary significance for pashaBiceps. Its story dates back to 2014-2015, when a wealthy fan using the pseudonym "motar2k" made large donations to the CS:GO community, gifting pasha tens of thousands of dollars in cash and multiple rare skins to help him purchase an apartment as he was about to become a father. Pasha kept this Karambit Fade as a memento, while selling off other items. After many years, he decided to "let go" of this "priceless treasure" to support the ongoing children's cancer charity campaign in Poland.

pashaBiceps posted on social media: "Poland is currently running the largest charity campaign to help young cancer warriors. Over 38 million Zloty has been raised so far. We can still do more." He called on the esports community to unite and contribute.

This auction was an important part of a charity livestream event. The event started with a modest goal of about 500,000 Zloty, and after 9 days of uninterrupted YouTube livestreaming, it quickly evolved into a rare charitable event in Polish and even European internet history. The event attracted numerous Polish celebrities, athletes, musicians, and international observers, with peak concurrent viewership exceeding 1.5 million.

According to the latest reports, the livestream has raised over 250 million Polish Zloty (approximately 59 million Euros or 68.9 million US dollars), successfully breaking the previous charity livestream fundraising record held by MrBeast and others. 100% of the funds will be directly used by the cancer foundation to support the cancer treatment of children and their families, with no commissions deducted.

Many netizens have expressed that this "one of the most iconic knives in CS history" has continued its legend in the best possible way.
