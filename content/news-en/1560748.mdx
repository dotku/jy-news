He said that his feelings for his wife are this kind of deeply ingrained, destined affection that he will never be able to let go of in his lifetime.

Luo Yonghao said: "There's a term now called physiological attraction, and I'm lucky enough to have that with my wife. Some friends ask, 'Does she have a physiological attraction to you?' No."

He also explained, "But I don't regret it, because I have a lot of love in my heart, it's overflowing, so I have a constant supply to give to her. She lacked love growing up due to her experiences, and the two of us have a very good internal cycle."

Although little is known about Luo Yonghao's wife, it is not difficult to see from the details he has repeatedly revealed publicly that the two have a very harmonious relationship in private.

As early as 2020, Luo Yonghao accepted a nearly 8-hour in-depth interview with "People" magazine, covering topics such as personality, wealth, reading experiences, family marriage, live-streaming e-commerce, debt repayment of hundreds of millions, and the failure of his Hammer startup.

At that time, when talking about his wife, Luo Yonghao once said bluntly: "As long as I smell her scent, such as the scent in her hair, I almost feel like I'm inhaling a therapeutic drug."

He also joked: "People say that keeping a cat is called 'cat therapy,' so I sometimes joke with her and say, 'Let me absorb some of your energy,' and she laughs. This is definitely not (a psychological effect), because I have verified it repeatedly, she is definitely functional."

It was precisely because of this straightforward and down-to-earth statement that the humorous meme "Luo Yonghao goes home to absorb his wife" quickly spread widely online.
