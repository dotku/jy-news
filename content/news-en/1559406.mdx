In terms of performance, the laptop is equipped with an Intel Core Ultra X7 358H processor, adopting the new Panther Lake architecture. It features a 16-core, 16-thread design, including 4 performance cores, 8 efficiency cores, and 4 low-power efficient cores, with a maximum turbo frequency of up to 4.8 GHz, easily handling various high-intensity office scenarios.

Regarding the screen, the laptop is equipped with a 3A-OLED display supporting a 120Hz VRR variable refresh rate and a peak brightness of 500 nits HDR.

The screen also utilizes a "three-proof" technology, significantly reducing glare, fingerprints, and stain interference, keeping the screen clean and clear even after prolonged use.

At the same time, the ThinkPad X1 Carbon Aura AI 2026 pioneered the "Star Track Architecture," releasing 18% of the motherboard space. This not only enhances the body's rigidity but also optimizes the internal thermal layout.

The fan area has increased by up to 81%, significantly improving heat dissipation efficiency. Coupled with upgraded cooling technology, it can quickly export heat from inside the body, ensuring stable temperature control during high-load operation, avoiding performance throttling or discomfort due to overheating.

In terms of battery life, it features a built-in 58 Wh high-density large-capacity battery, paired with VRR intelligent scheduling technology, which can intelligently adjust power consumption based on usage scenarios. Under MM30 standards, the maximum battery life can reach 16 hours, and local video playback can last up to 22 hours.

The laptop also comes with a 65W gallium nitride charger, supporting intelligent charging function, which can intelligently learn user habits, accurately adjust charging time and speed, effectively extending battery life while reducing battery loss.

In terms of network connectivity, the laptop offers an optional built-in LTE wireless network card, eliminating reliance on mobile hotspots. Whether in business cars, high-speed trains, or airports, it can quickly connect to the network, instantly launching video conferences, and avoiding work progress delays due to network lag or disconnection.

In addition, the Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Carbon Aura AI 2026 is equipped with Tianxi Personal Super Intelligence (Tianxi AI). Tianxi AI possesses five capabilities: perception, thinking, memory, planning, and execution, and achieves three core upgrades based on a "end-cloud integrated" hybrid architecture: all-domain knowledge base construction, end-cloud security authentication, and multi-end collaborative experience. With the help of the MCP intelligent agent scheduling protocol and the A2A networking architecture, Tianxi AI has gathered more than 5000 domain agents and AI applications.
