  Microsoft will remain OpenAI's primary cloud service partner, and OpenAI's
  products will be prioritized for release on the Azure platform.

Microsoft will continue to have a license to use intellectual property related to OpenAI models and products, valid until 2032.

OpenAI's revenue sharing arrangement with Microsoft will continue until 2030.

As a significant shareholder, Microsoft will continue to be directly involved in OpenAI's growth.
