European airlines urged the EU on Tuesday to take emergency measures to address the fallout from the Iran conflict, such as monitoring the aviation fuel supply situation.

Germany has participated in the release of international strategic oil reserves. According to the Ministry of Economic Affairs, Germany's share of the release is 4.9%, equivalent to 2.647 million tons, including 50,000 tons of aviation fuel.
