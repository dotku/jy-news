Lisuan Technology's previously launched 7G100 series GPUs are based on a 6nm process. Unlike the common licensing model on the market, this series of graphics cards are completely independently designed by Lisuan, from the computing core to the instruction set.

This series of GPUs is based on the self-developed TrueGPU Tiantu architecture, and is equipped with a self-developed instruction set and software stack. This approach of not relying on third-party IP licensing means that Lisuan Technology completely masters the underlying autonomy of the GPU architecture, avoiding potential technological sanctions risks.

In performance testing, the Lisuan eXtreme series graphics cards performed impressively. They scored as high as 26800 in the FireStrike test, and also reached a high score of 2268 in the Steel Nomad test, which reflects overall strength.

In comparison, the mainstream NVIDIA model RTX 4060 scores approximately 2302 in the same mode.

In terms of actual gaming experience, the performance of this series of graphics cards running the domestically produced 3A masterpiece "Black Myth: Wukong" is surprising. At 1080P resolution and high image quality settings, the average frame rate can be stably maintained above 70 frames, with extremely high smoothness.

Another highly anticipated domestically produced 3A game, "Bright Memory: Infinite," can also run smoothly on this graphics card. This excellent measured performance fully demonstrates Lisuan's graphics cards' dual compatibility advantages in both high-performance hardware and complex software environments.

In addition to gaming and entertainment, Lisuan graphics cards have also precisely entered the current hot field of artificial intelligence. The Lisuan eXtreme series graphics cards can fully meet the needs of personal AIPC for mainstream large models, and the application prospects are very broad.
