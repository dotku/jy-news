Prior to this update, RTX 5080 cloud computing power was limited to a select few games. Now, with this upgrade, Ultimate members can enjoy significantly improved frame rates and visual quality in thousands of games by default.

After unlocking RTX 5080 performance, Ultimate members can enjoy up to 120 FPS of incredibly smooth visuals at 5K resolution, or activate a super-low-latency competitive mode at 360 FPS at 1080p resolution.

The cloud-synchronized NVIDIA DLSS 4 frame generation technology and Reflex low-latency technology will further enhance image detail and compress operation latency. In games that have already been adapted, such as *Black Myth: Wukong* and *Cyberpunk 2077*, players will be able to experience better PC ray tracing effects.

In addition to this nuclear-level performance update, GeForce NOW also announced 16 games that will be added to the library in May. Among them, the highly anticipated exclusive title is *Forza Motorsport 6* from Playground Games, which will be available on the cloud on its launch day.

Another highly anticipated stealth action game, *007: First Dawn*, will also join the cloud gaming library on its release date of May 27th.

For players who don't want to purchase expensive physical graphics cards but still desire to experience flagship-level performance and AAA titles, GeForce NOW’s latest move directly raises the standard for cloud gaming experiences to a level that competes directly with high-end local PCs.
