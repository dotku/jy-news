In terms of sound quality, the headset's driver unit references the core technology of the MLDR-MV1 open-style recording studio monitor headphones. In addition, the product is equipped with an adjustable cardioid directional microphone, capable of achieving clear and natural sound capture.

Sony collaborated with sound designers from PlayStation Studios to customize an "RPG/Adventure" equalizer profile for the INZONE H6 Air. This profile is exclusively tuned for RPG and adventure games, enhancing sound clarity, depth, and environmental details, more accurately restoring the intended listening experience of the game.

Users can also connect the headset via the included USB-C audio box to obtain virtual 7.1 channel surround sound and 360-degree spatial audio, further enhancing the gaming immersion experience.
