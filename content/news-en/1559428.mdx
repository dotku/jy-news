A video released by netizens shows that after the Seres Z7 and Z7T were unveiled, the AITO M6 was presented next. However, after the other vehicles drove off the stage, one Seres vehicle remained stationary, leading netizens to speculate about a possible malfunction.

HarmonyOS ZhiXing staff responded, "In order to allow everyone to better experience today's products, we will simultaneously display four new vehicles on stage. Thank you for your understanding and support, and please do not over-interpret or spread information."
