Tesla CEO Musk has stated that Tesla owners will eventually be able to "basically do anything through voice commands."

In addition, Musk's Grok voice function has been launched in the Grok application, offering two different voice styles (Ara and Grok) for users to choose from based on their preferences.

Furthermore, the function allows users to add custom instructions for a more personalized interactive experience. Users can easily record and share conversation content, enhancing the convenience and fun of social interaction. Complex tasks and queries can be completed with simple voice commands.
