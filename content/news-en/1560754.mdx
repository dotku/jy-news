You don't understand why I'm asking all employees to open social media accounts, and some people say this is just multi-level marketing, that Dreame lacks technology.

You don't understand the importance of compound abilities to people!

Engineers are naturally good at dealing with complex parameters and solving thousands of technical problems, but they are not good at communicating with people, nor are they good at explaining things clearly! I want to train everyone to speak plainly, to use simple and easy-to-understand language to explain their products and technology clearly! And to be able to learn from interactive feedback! What exactly can attract people, and what can others understand at first listen!

Some people also asked me, if they already have hundreds of thousands of fans, will they be able to receive hundreds of thousands of yuan in rewards when joining Dreame? No, that won't happen, because that's still a single ability.

If you already have hundreds of thousands of fans, it means you've learned to interact with people! If you join Dreame at this point, and you learn to write hundreds of thousands of lines of code, then I will give you hundreds of thousands of yuan in rewards! Because that is a compound ability!

As the age of AI arrives, single abilities are easily replaced by AI, and only those who master compound abilities will be more competitive! They will be able to control AI, control complex systems, lead larger teams, and achieve outstanding performance and cross-field innovation that ordinary people cannot achieve!

If you don't understand my good intentions, don't think others are foolish. If I can repeatedly propose such seemingly unreasonable requests, and Dreame has so many high-IQ and high-ability employees who resolutely implement them, don't be quick to deny it. It's likely that the one who is wrong is you, who doesn't understand!

Previously, Yu Hao posted that all employees should open accounts on all platforms: Every Dreame employee should open accounts on all platforms! Use 15 minutes of video each day to post three videos introducing the products and technology you are working on! Or introduce the various selling points of the company's products! Core technology and innovation! Just the Dreame employees themselves would have at least 20,000 accounts!

He also stated that a reward of 10,000 yuan will be given for 10,000 fans, 50,000 yuan for 50,000 fans, and 100,000 yuan for 100,000 fans.
