Passengers only need to complete the reservation and payment online through the 12306 client. To ensure travel safety and order within the carriages, bicycles must pass station security checks and be stored in designated areas after proper packaging. This measure greatly facilitates cycling enthusiasts.

According to the plan of the National Railway Group, the integration of railways and tourism will be further deepened in 2026. Relevant departments plan to operate more than 2,500 tourist trains throughout the year and carefully create more than 50 high-quality tourist routes, making the railway network a convenient link connecting major scenic spots.

To stimulate the tourism market, the railway department has also launched a series of benefit measures for the people. From the end of April to the end of June, 179 special tourist trains will be launched successively, and railway tourism consumption vouchers will be distributed during April and May, providing real subsidies to reduce travel costs for passengers.

Specifically for the elderly, the railway department has also prepared exclusive benefits. From May 30th to June 30th, Chinese citizens aged 60 and over can enjoy discounts on fares when traveling on some high-speed train sets during weekdays, encouraging more seniors to travel during off-peak hours.

The implementation of these new policies aims to help more passengers realize their wish of traveling around China by train. By continuously developing new service growth points, the railway department not only injects vitality into the tourism economy but also provides strong impetus for the development of the silver economy and the sports and leisure industry.
