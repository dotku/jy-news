From "Have you ever envied someone who was very handsome?" and "You actually like the public, don't you?" to "Are you the type of person who gets impatient when they see others are weak?" and "Why are you worried about a world after you're gone?", a series of increasingly pressing questions left Luo Yonghao sighing helplessly.

After repeated questioning, Cai Kangyong also frankly revealed his true feelings, saying: "You think I came here for nothing? Actually, I've always been interested in you."

Luo Yonghao also playfully joked about this collaboration, saying that recording a podcast with a top host like Cai Kangyong requires staying "vigilant," otherwise, the roles might be reversed.

In addition, another highlight reel showed Cai Kangyong sharing his unique wisdom in dealing with the world.

In another clip, Cai Kangyong talked about his past experiences and insights from "Kangxi Came," sharing that when faced with praise from others, one should accept it without being indulgent, politely thank them without taking it too seriously. Only then can one remain calm and unfazed when facing criticism and accusations from the outside world.

At the same time, Cai Kangyong also generously shared his transparent views on death and life.

He said, "You know you're going to die, and the world will no longer exist," and also lamented, "I think being with everyone for too long will make them realize they are getting old." Cai Kangyong believes this is a very damaging thing.

Throughout the conversation, Cai Kangyong spoke calmly and lightly, but every sentence hit the nail on the head, even making Luo Yonghao exclaim: "Cai Kangyong always speaks calmly, but every word carries a lot of weight."

It is understood that the full interview will be officially launched at 12:00 noon on April 29th. This two-way, soulful, and interesting conversation is worth looking forward to.
