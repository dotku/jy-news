This high-priced train is not an ordinary passenger train, but a high-end customized tourism product operated by a tourism company stationed on the 12306 platform.

The itinerary departs from and returns to Xining, covering multiple core scenic spots in Xinjiang throughout the journey. It adopts an all-inclusive model, including all services such as transportation, accommodation, catering, and sightseeing. It also features a dedicated butler, a photographer accompanying the entire journey, and special themed catering, focusing on private, high-end, and immersive travel experiences.

A staff member of the operator stated that the train is positioned for the high-end market, with hardware and software benchmarked against high-end hotel standards. There are no shopping or optional activities arranged throughout the journey. Local reception services are provided by its own team, adopting a small reception model of one room per vehicle to ensure privacy and comfort.

The train has only 38 independent cabins, accommodating approximately 76 people at full capacity, with no multi-person layouts. Public spaces are ample, with some suites covering up to 22 square meters, equipped with smart bathrooms and other high-end facilities. The overall configuration is at a high level among similar products in China.

Regarding the high price question, the operator explained that the 200,000 yuan is the standard price for adults, covering all services for the entire 17-day journey, not just the train ticket.

Current sales are good, with two top-level suites priced at over one million yuan already sold.

The train needs to be approved by the railway department for routes and schedules in advance, and will depart on time after the plan is confirmed, without relying on group bookings to proceed.

12306 customer service stated that these tourist trains are applied for by travel agencies and are not directly operated by the railway department. The ticket prices are independent of ordinary trains, and relevant information can be consulted through the operator's contact information on the platform.
