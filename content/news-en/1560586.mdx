Tongde emphasized in the statement that this adjustment is merely a long-planned global brand integration initiative within the group, and not a brand shutdown or business termination. The GALAX brand will not cease operations and will continue to invest in the research, development, production, and after-sales service of hardware products. The existing product iteration roadmap will continue as originally planned and will not be affected by this management adjustment.

It is understood that GALAX has been a core brand under Tongde Group since 2007. This reform consolidates the management rights of the three product lines – GALAX, KFA2, and HOF (Hall of Fame) – under the centralized control of Tongde Group headquarters.

The core purpose of this move is to coordinate global brand layout, optimize the global supply chain system, integrate R&D resources, and improve synergy efficiency between brands, further strengthening the group's overall competitiveness.

Officially confirmed, the three brands GALAX, KFA2, and HOF will all be retained and continue to develop independently. This is only an optimization and integration of internal management models, and ordinary consumers will hardly be affected. Only the after-sales service contact channels in a few regions such as Brazil will be changed to Tongde official customer service.

In addition to the Tongde Group announcement, GALAX also simultaneously released a separate official statement, reiterating that the brand will not leave the GPU industry and all business operations will continue as normal.

GALAX stated that, relying on Tongde Group’s in-depth collaboration for many years, this unified management is a strategic upgrade aimed at amplifying the brand’s global influence, not reducing business. In the future, the brand will continue to launch new graphics card products and continue to provide high-performance hardware products for gaming players and creative design users.

In conclusion, all rumors of delisting and production stoppage are unfounded. Tongde and GALAX will rely on unified management to accelerate technological innovation and product updates, and all classic graphics card series will continue to iterate, with long-term development plans remaining stable.
