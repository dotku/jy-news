After the race, Zhang Xue, the founder of Zhang Xue Motorcycle, posted: "Who disagrees that we're awesome?"

It is understood that in the previous Portugal station, rider Valentin Debise won both races, taking the historic first championship for the Chinese motorcycle brand in this top international event, achieving a historic breakthrough.

Notably, he finished nearly 4 seconds ahead when crossing the finish line in the first championship race, breaking the decades-long dominance of international giants such as Ducati, Yamaha, and Kawasaki in this category.

Subsequently, at the Dutch station, Debise finished 4th and 7th in the two races.

“Why do we participate in such competitions? To prove that our motorcycles, our mass-produced Chinese motorcycles, can win championships in world-class events,” said Zhang Xue.
