The game, which officially launched earlier this month, has now been renamed "Grand Car Racing."

This renaming requirement was not extended to other platforms; the game remains listed as "Gran Carismo" on the Steam and Nintendo eShop stores.

This renaming was likely rushed: although the game's name has been updated in the PS Store, the store tile's artwork has not been updated accordingly and still displays the old name.

While a bit of a mess, the issue has been somewhat resolved.
