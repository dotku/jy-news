This crossover interaction between the "table tennis superstar" and the "AI godfather" has sparked heated discussion among netizens.

Notably, Huang Renxun is also a table tennis enthusiast and previously won third place in the US Open Table Tennis tournament.

Huang Renxun had previously revealed that he particularly loves playing table tennis, loves it so much that he wants to play every day. He even sleeps on the floor of the table tennis club on weekends so he can keep playing.
