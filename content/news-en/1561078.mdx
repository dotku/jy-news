After receiving the request for help, the Jiande Public Security Bureau's patrol special police detachment immediately dispatched professional drone pilots, carrying two drones into the mountains to search, one of which was equipped with thermal imaging equipment.

The search in the deep mountains was extremely difficult. After 3 hours and using 6 sets of batteries, the pilots finally captured a weak thermal signal near the Lihutang Reservoir in the depths of the mountains.

Police and villagers immediately rushed to the scene and found the cow's reins tightly entangled in tree roots and bushes, trapping it in place and unable to move, meaning it had been unable to eat or drink for 20 full days.

At this point, it was skin and bones, so weak it could barely stand, but its will to survive remained strong. After being rescued, the "Niu Jianqiang" quickly began to recover its diet and gained 30 jin just two days after being found.
