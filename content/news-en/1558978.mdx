In terms of core imaging, the Huawei AI Glasses are equipped with a 1200-megapixel ultra-sensitive camera and a 1/2.8-inch large-bottom sensor.

It is also the first AI glasses in the industry to support the HDR Vivid photography standard, with photo resolution up to 4096×3072 and supports recording videos up to 1920×1440/30fps.

In terms of shooting response, the glasses feature a 0.7-second ultra-clear AI flash capture function, support AI composition intelligent correction, and can achieve automatic correction of horizontal tilt of ±20°.

In terms of AI interaction, the Huawei AI Glasses are equipped with a self-developed AI glasses chip, built-in Xiaoyi intelligent assistant, and support two wake-up methods: voice wake-up and pressing the AI shortcut key on the left side of the frame.

In payment scenarios, users can wake up Xiaoyi through voice to directly use the glasses to scan codes to initiate Alipay payments, and voiceprint verification technology ensures payment security.

In terms of wearing experience, the Huawei AI Glasses adopt lightweight new materials and precision stacking technology, with the temples as thin as 6.25mm and the frame as light as 35.5 grams.
