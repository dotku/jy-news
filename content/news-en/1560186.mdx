Simba stated that if Hao Taotao and his business team continue to make negative comments and slander Xin Selection, he will launch a full-scale attack on the entire team and is willing to go down with them.

According to Simba's livestream revelations, when Hao Taotao left Xin Selection, he abandoned a department worth over ten billion yuan annually for over a month. After leaving his position, he continued to speak negatively about Xin Selection in front of cooperating merchants, claiming that Xin Selection is now failing and finished.

In addition, Simba publicly offered a reward to all merchants within the ecosystem for evidence of Hao Taotao's crimes, stating that he would pay 1 million yuan for any evidence that could increase Hao Taotao's sentence by one year.

Public information shows that Hao Taotao once served as the head of the Xin Selection Group's product center, responsible for core areas such as supply chain, product selection, and quality control, and was fully responsible for the group's core commercialization business, managing annual sales of over 20 billion yuan.

In January of this year, Xin Selection Group officially released an announcement confirming Hao Taotao's departure and ending their cooperation.

After leaving his position, Hao Taotao chose to cooperate with Dandan, another streamer who left Xin Selection and has conflicts with Simba, taking over a 7 million-follower account given to him by Dandan.
