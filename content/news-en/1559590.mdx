Looking at the cumulative sales for the entire year of 2026, "Resident Evil: Revelations" remains the best-selling game in the US market. "MLB The Show 26" ranked second, "WWE 2K26" third, and "NBA 2K26" and "Call of Duty: Black Ops 7" ranked fourth and fifth respectively.

In terms of hardware, the Switch 2 became the best-selling game console in the US in March. "Pokémon Pokopia" was described as a "system-level hit," strongly boosting sales of the Nintendo console. PS5 ranked second.
