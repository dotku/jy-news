Think of datacenter networking like water moving through pipes. 

 

Traditional optical cables move data through a narrow, fast stream of light. While Microsoft’s new MicroLED system sends data through thousands of channels at once – more like a wide, slow-moving river carrying the same volume. 

 

The result: a new approach that researchers estimate could use about 50% less energy than mainstream laser-based optical cables, while supporting the massive data flows behind AI and cloud services. Learn how: https://msft.it/6001QoewP
