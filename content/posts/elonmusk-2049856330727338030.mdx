The Tesla Model X was the fastest-selling used vehicle in the U.S. in March 2026, according to a new study by iSeeCars.



On average, it found a buyer in 26 days, compared to the typical car, which usually lingers on the market for about two months.



Other Tesla models are also selling faster than most used cars. The figure for the Model Y is 35 days. The figure for the Model S is 35 days.
