在用户提出健康相关问题时，Perplexity Health 可以综合电子病历、化验结果以及可穿戴设备采集的数据生成回答。配合公司的 AI 代理工具“Perplexity Computer”，相关代理还能够基于个人健康状况生成定制化的健身计划、营养方案等。目前，Perplexity Health 在 Computer 端率先向美国地区的 Pro 与 Max 订阅用户开放。

据介绍，Perplexity Health 使用的医学信息来源包括临床指南和同行评议期刊等“优质医学文献”。公司同时成立了由医生、科研人员和数字健康技术专家组成的“Perplexity Health 顾问委员会”，以循证医学标准为依据，对产品决策、内容质量以及临床安全防护措施进行审查和“压力测试”。

在数据接入方面，Perplexity Health 已支持与 Apple 健康打通，可整合 Apple Watch 及录入至 Apple 健康应用的相关数据。此外，服务还兼容 Fitbit、Ultrahuman、Withings 等设备平台，并可连接来自超过 170 万家医疗服务机构的电子健康记录；对 Oura 和 Function 的集成支持则将于后续推出。

Perplexity 强调，所有健康数据均采用加密存储，并设置严格的访问控制机制，用户可随时管理或删除相关信息。公司同时承诺，健康数据不会被用于训练 AI 模型，也不会出售给第三方。

值得一提的是，Perplexity 是继 OpenAI 之后第二家与 Apple 健康进行集成的 AI 公司。此前，OpenAI 已在 2026 年初推出包含 Apple 健康支持的 ChatGPT Health 功能。

---

When users ask health-related questions, Perplexity Health can generate answers by combining electronic medical records, lab results, and data collected from wearable devices. Coupled with the company's AI agent tool "Perplexity Computer," related agents can also generate customized fitness plans, nutritional programs, and more based on individual health status. Currently, Perplexity Health is first available to Pro and Max subscribers in the US on the Computer end.

It is reported that the medical information sources used by Perplexity Health include "high-quality medical literature" such as clinical guidelines and peer-reviewed journals. The company has also established a "Perplexity Health Advisory Board" consisting of doctors, researchers, and digital health technology experts to review and "stress test" product decisions, content quality, and clinical safety protection measures based on evidence-based medicine standards.

In terms of data access, Perplexity Health has already supported integration with Apple Health, allowing it to integrate data from Apple Watch and data entered into the Apple Health app. In addition, the service is also compatible with device platforms such as Fitbit, Ultrahuman, and Withings, and can connect to electronic health records from more than 1.7 million healthcare institutions; integration support for Oura and Function will be launched subsequently.

Perplexity emphasizes that all health data is encrypted and stored with strict access control mechanisms, and users can manage or delete relevant information at any time. The company also promises that health data will not be used to train AI models or sold to third parties.

It is worth mentioning that Perplexity is the second AI company after OpenAI to integrate with Apple Health. Previously, OpenAI had already announced the launch of ChatGPT Health with Apple Health support in early 2026.
