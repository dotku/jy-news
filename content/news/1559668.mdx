summary_en: >-
  U.S. Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick said on Thursday that, as of now, only
  one person has been approved for the "Gold Card" visa program launched by
  Trump. The program allows foreigners to pay $1 million to obtain U.S.
  residency. Lutnick did not disclose the identity of the approved applicant. He
  stated during testimony before a House committee that "hundreds" of other
  foreign applicants are having their visa applications reviewed.
