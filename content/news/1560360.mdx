  Today, the State Taxation Administration announced a tax evasion case.
  Investigation revealed that "exploring shops" internet celebrity Bai Bing
  evaded a total of 9.1118 million yuan in personal income tax, value-added tax,

公开信息显示，白冰抖音账号“MR-白冰”当前粉丝数达4041万，上一次更新时间为2天前。新浪科技发现，目前该账号已被抖音平台禁言，页面提示“该用户因违反相关法律法规和政策，已被禁言”。
