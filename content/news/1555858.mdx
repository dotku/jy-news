目前，执行此次任务的四名宇航员已经顺利抵达佛罗里达州的肯尼迪航天中心。他们正在那里紧锣密鼓地开展发射前的最后阶段工作，着手对每一个技术细节进行最终确认，确保飞船与地面系统在实战中能够万无一失。

这支精英机组由三名美国宇航员里德怀斯曼、维克多格洛弗、克里斯蒂娜·科赫，以及来自加拿大航天局的宇航员杰里米·汉森共同组成。

按照当前的发射序列计划，最早将于4月1日正式启程。

---

Currently, the four astronauts assigned to this mission have successfully arrived at the Kennedy Space Center in Florida. They are there intensely conducting the final stages of pre-launch work, beginning the final confirmation of every technical detail to ensure the spacecraft and ground systems operate flawlessly in a real-world scenario.

This elite crew consists of three American astronauts – Reid Wiseman, Victor Glover, and Christina Koch – and Canadian Space Agency astronaut Jeremy Hansen.

According to the current launch sequence plan, the mission is scheduled to officially commence as early as April 1st.
